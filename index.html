Your Questions Answered

What should I expect during my first visit?

Dr. Prithvi conducts a thorough skin/hair analysis, discusses your lifestyle, and creates a customized treatment plan tailored to your goals.

Are cosmetic procedures safe?

Absolutely. All procedures at Esthetique are performed or supervised by Dr. Prithvi Shetty using US-FDA approved equipment and medical-grade products.

How many PRP sessions are needed for hair fall?

Typically, 4-6 sessions spaced a month apart are recommended for visible results, though this varies based on individual hair density and condition.

Do you treat skin allergies?

Yes, we manage all clinical dermatology issues including allergies, rashes, fungal infections, and chronic conditions like psoriasis.
